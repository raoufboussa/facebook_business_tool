Ads Analytics Tool

About Us

Our service represents a dedicated tool which supports a marketing manager by gathering all the necessary information in a single place thus he can be informed of the business's state of a client (pages and advertising campaigns) ,also it allows to analyze them and detect information allowing to build for each customer a digital strategy according to the different circumstances and then improve it in an effective way.

WHAT OUR PLATEFORM OFFERS

STATESTICS OF FACEBOOK PAGES

You can collect important data and statestics concerning the pages owned by each client and gather them in an interactive dashboard,making it possible to evaluate the different actions in order to have an effective page in that niche.

STATESTICS OF ADVERTISING CAMPAIGNS

You can collect important data and statistics concerning all the advertising campaigns launched and gather them in a dashboard,making it possible to carry out an analysis to improve the configuration of the future campaigns.

INFORMATION FOR NEW DECISIONS

You can use the data collected to get an idea of ​​what is working and what is not working, in order to make decisions especially what has to do with the configuration of advertising campaigns, because our tool offers the functionality of offering the best configuration for a new campaign based on the experiences of the campaigns already lunched.

WHAT MAKES OUR TOOL USEFUL

Building a digital strategy can take a lot of time especially for new clients and it can be challenging to find a suitable tool that provides the necessary functionalities in a user friendly way. This is why we are proposing this new tool that is going to revolutionize digital marketing.

WE OFFER DIFFERENT LEVELS OF DETAIL

In ower analytics, sometimes we want to have a general idea of how our marketing is doing. But other times it is necessary to dig deeper and understand what happened on the campaign level. This is why we offer these two levels on granularity for Facebook Ads.

WE AIM AT MAKING THINGS VERY SIMPLE

Have you ever tried a tool and did not have an idea of where to start ? YES, this happened to us too and the good news are that our tool is destined for business users and so it is very intuitive and easy to use.

WE WILL CONTINUE WORKING ON MAKING OUR TOOL BETTER

Right now, we use the most used plateforms among digital marketers, but in the future more plateforms will be added and other features will also be integrated in our plateform.

HOW CAN I IMPROOVE A DIGITAL MARKETING STRATEGY

As we have mentioned earlier, ease of use had been a very important factor during the design of our tool and this is why you can use the tool to improve the marketing stratigy in simple steps.

STEP 1: CREATE AN ACCOUNT IN OUR PLATEFORM.

To guarantee your security and the privacy of your data, our clients need to open an account and have their own password so that they can access their workspace.

STEP 2: CONNECT TO YOUR ACCOUNTS AND CONNECT YOUR PAGES.

Using very secured protocols proposed by Facebook, you can connect to your accounts then connect our pages so that you can have all your clients in your workspace and from there you can start working.

STEP 3: CHOOSE AN ADD ACOUNT AND START WORKING ON YOUR STRATEGY.

The last step is to check your pages's and campagnes's analytics and make better decisions for your future campagnes.
